# Last Ride: Zombie Car Survival

## Game Description
The zombie apocalypse has arrived, and your car is your lifeline. In this intense survival game, you must navigate through a world overrun by the undead while managing your resources, health, and vehicle condition from the safety of your car.

## How to Play
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click "Start Game" to begin your survival journey
3. Manage your health, hunger, thirst, and energy levels
4. Maintain your vehicle's fuel and condition
5. Make strategic decisions to survive as long as possible

## Key Features
- **Survival Management**: Monitor critical stats while making life-or-death decisions
- **Vehicle Mechanics**: Your car is your mobile fortress - keep it running!
- **Dynamic Locations**: Explore areas with varying zombie populations and resources
- **Story Events**: Encounter survivors and face unexpected situations
- **Global Leaderboard**: Compete worldwide to see who survives longest
- **Day/Night Cycle**: Time progression affects danger and strategy

## Controls
- **Mouse**: Click buttons to perform actions
- **Interface**: All actions are button-based for easy gameplay

## System Requirements
- **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (modern browsers)
- **Resolution**: 1366x768 minimum, 1920x1080 recommended
- **Internet**: Required for global leaderboard features

## Gameplay Tips
- Monitor your fuel levels - a broken car means certain death
- Scavenging is risky but necessary for supplies
- Rest during safer times to recover energy
- Every choice matters - plan your survival strategy

## Objective
Survive as many days as possible while managing your health, hunger, thirst, vehicle condition, and fuel. The longer you survive, the higher your score!

Can you outlast the apocalypse, or will your last ride end in the zombie wasteland?
